Recipe Of The Week: Red, White, and Blue Strawberry Shortcake

Having trouble finding a great dessert to make for Memorial Day? This Strawberry Shortcake is an American Favorite!
Ingredients:

  • 1 (18.25 oz) package of yellow cake mix
  • 1 (8 oz) container of frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 pint of fresh blueberries, rinsed and drained
  • 2 pints of fresh strawberries, rinsed and sliced

    • Directions:
  • Prepare cake according to package directions and bake in a 9x13 inch pan. Cool completely before continuing.
  • Frost cake with whipped topping. Place blueberries in a square in the cornor of the pan, and place the sliced strawberries as stripes to resemble the American Flag. Place in refrigerator until serving time.
